Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At DV Dashboard, we believe that your data belongs to you. We have designed our bot and dashboard with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We operate on the principle of data minimization—if we don't need it to make the bot work, we don't collect it.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell personal information about our users. Our bot is built strictly for the purpose of enhancing your Discord server experience. Specifically:

  • We do not log the content of your private messages or server conversations.
  • We do not track your activity across other websites or services.
  • We do not store PII (Personally Identifiable Information) beyond what is strictly necessary to identify your session for the dashboard.

2. How We Use Information

The only information the DV Dashboard accesses is the metadata required for the bot to function, such as your Discord user ID (to authenticate your login) and the IDs of the servers you manage (to show your dashboard options). This data is used solely to facilitate your management of your server settings.

3. No Data Sharing

We maintain a strict "No-Sharing" policy. Because we do not collect personal data, there is no data to share. We do not work with advertisers, third-party data brokers, or external analytics firms. Your privacy is not a commodity to us.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that your interactions with the DV Dashboard remain secure. Our code is designed for "good usage only," and we perform regular audits to ensure our bot remains a safe, helpful, and non-intrusive addition to your Discord server.

5. User Control

Since we do not store your data, there is nothing for you to "request" or "delete." You have complete control: you can remove the bot from your server at any time, and you can revoke our access through your Discord Authorized Apps settings. Once the bot is removed, all interaction ceases immediately.

6. Changes to this Policy

If we ever decide to update our privacy practices, we will post the changes here and notify our users via our official support channels. We will never retroactively weaken the privacy protections provided to our users.

"Privacy is not a feature, it's a foundation."
